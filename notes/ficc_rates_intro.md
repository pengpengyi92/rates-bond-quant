# FICC Rates Intro

FICC means:

```text
Fixed Income
Currencies
Commodities
```

This project focuses only on:

```text
Fixed Income -> Interest Rate Bonds
```

It does not cover credit bonds, FX, commodities, swaps, or derivatives in the
MVP.

## Why Rates Bonds Matter

Interest-rate bonds are directly linked to:

- central bank policy
- inflation expectations
- liquidity conditions
- fiscal supply
- duration demand
- risk sentiment

The most basic rates-bond quant question is:

```text
If yields move by X basis points, how much does the bond price change?
```

This leads to duration and convexity.
